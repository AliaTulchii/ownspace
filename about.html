About Own Space Residential Complex

Own Space is a premium investment that has no analogues in Poland. Height 200 m, designed using the latest technology by world famous architects. This residential building offers magnificent views of the city, prestigious premises, amenities for residents and a unique recreation area. This is not only the highest residential investment in the European Union, but also the highest level of luxury in the heart of Warsaw, as well as a safe and valuable investment of capital in the real estate market.
